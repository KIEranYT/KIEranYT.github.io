 About us

   

   BaKa is a gaming team and YouTube community. We are trying hard to grow and get bigger. You will see us playing games like Fortnite, WarFrame, Super Smash Bros, Brawlhalla and others. You will  also see us discussing big topics in the gaming and YouTube community, such as game and console releases. We cover lots of Nintendo news and react to videos as well. However we mostly just  post gameplay videos. We stream as a team a lot and will have a lot of fun. Don't miss the fun!
